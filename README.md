# Aim Trainer
A high-performance precision trainer to help with your accuracy.

AimAssist delivers a seamless, installation-free experience for gamers looking to level up. By combining HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this trainer provides the responsiveness needed to truly test your limits.

No setup required and you can play instantly in any browser.

This was inspired from my cyclemates and tailored for i.c.stars gamers and anyone looking to refine their accuracy or unwind with a simple, engaging challenge!
***

##  Features

*   **3 Difficulty Modes**  
    Easy, Medium, and Hard — each with unique target sizes, speeds, and point values

*   **Smooth Target Movement**  
    Targets glide between random positions at 60 FPS using `requestAnimationFrame`

*   **Live HUD**  
    Real-time display of:
    *   Score
    *   Streak
    *   Accuracy
    *   Countdown timer

*   **2× Streak Bonus**  
    Land **5 hits in a row** to activate a **5‑second double‑points window**

*   **Grade System**  
    End-of-round accuracy rating: **S / A / B / C / D / F**

*   **End‑of‑Round Stats**
    *   Final score
    *   Hits & misses
    *   Accuracy
    *   Best streak
    *   2× bonus activations

*   **Tactical UI**  
    Dark, gamer-style theme with glowing elements

*   **Fully Responsive**  
    Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

***

##  How to Play

1.  Open `index.html` in your browser
2.  Select a difficulty: **Easy**, **Medium**, or **Hard**
3.  Click **Start** to begin
4.  Click the glowing target as quickly and accurately as possible
5.  Build a **5-hit streak** to activate **2× points**
6.  When time runs out, review your grade and statistics
